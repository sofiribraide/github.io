Background

Sofiri Gary Braide was born in Calabar, Nigeria on the 23rd of July, 1990. He is currently based in the city of Port Harcourt, Nigeria. With a wealth of knowledge, experience and expertise in the fields of computer science, graphic design and branding, Sofiri has had the pleasure to work with businesses and brands in various sectors; ranging from food processing to education to renewable energy.
